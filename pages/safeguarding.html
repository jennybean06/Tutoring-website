Safeguarding

Your child's safety is important. Whether the lesson is in-person or online, here are several ways to keep your child safe and ensure accountability.

    Please ensure that you are within listening distance of the lesson taking place.
    Your child should not be left alone in the house without a guardian or parent present.
    If teaching is taking place in a separate room, the door should always be open.
